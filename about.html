Our Story

Eden Contracting NY was founded with a simple mission: to provide New York City property owners with the highest quality sidewalk repair and concrete services at fair, transparent prices. Based in the Bronx and serving all five boroughs, we have built our reputation on expert craftsmanship, reliable service, and complete customer satisfaction.

Our team understands the unique challenges of NYC's urban environment. From the heavy foot traffic of Manhattan to the diverse residential communities of Queens, we tailor our services to meet the specific needs of each neighborhood and property type.
